GlobalLogic · Apple Watch Voice AI

R&D engagement for a voice-first Apple Watch companion for field workers. Hands are often occupied, so the Watch is the primary wrist surface. This is not a production App Store product. Delivery was a TestFlight demo under watchOS constraints.

watchOS could not host the realtime WebSocket and audio path the conversation needed, so the iPhone became a relay. Audio and socket traffic moved through the phone while the Watch kept the conversation UI. Structured responses from the AI assistant were interpreted on-device as project/domain command contracts and mapped to application and UI actions — not Apple App Intents. VelocityAI was evaluated through hands-on iOS delivery scenarios, from specification and design context through implementation workflows.

  • watchOS conversation UI under realtime audio and runtime constraints
  • iPhone relay for WebSocket/audio path and cross-device sync
  • structured AI command contracts → on-device application/UI actions
  • VelocityAI evaluation through hands-on iOS delivery scenarios
